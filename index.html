Demon Slayer Chronicles

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive, Tanjiro and his sister, Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.

Demon Slayer Characters

Tanjiro is kind by nature and is very brave and determined.

Nezuko is a demon but she prtoects humans and helps her brother Tanjiro.

Zenitsu is not very brave but has a kind heart and very good hearing.

Inosuke is very blunt but has good instincts and is always up for a challenge.
